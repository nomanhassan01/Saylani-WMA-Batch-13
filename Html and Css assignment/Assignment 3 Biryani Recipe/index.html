Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (or your choice of meat)
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 4 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water

Method

  1. Rinse the rice thoroughly and soak it for 30 minutes. Then, cook it in 4 cups of water with a pinch of salt until 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pan and fry the sliced onions until golden brown. Set half aside for garnishing.
  3. In the same pan, add cumin seeds, ginger-garlic paste, and green chilies. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and biryani masala. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add the chicken and cook until it changes color. Then add yogurt and mix well.
  6. Cover the pan and let the chicken cook on low heat until tender.
  7. Once the chicken is cooked, layer the semi-cooked rice over it. Sprinkle with coriander, mint, fried onions, and saffron milk (if using).
  8. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for about 20-25 minutes.
  9. Once done, gently mix the rice and chicken together before serving hot.
Enjoy your homemade biryani!
